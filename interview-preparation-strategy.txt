Interview Preparation Strategy

    Practice Coding:
        Implement common algorithms and data structures
        Build mini-projects showcasing different patterns
        Practice explaining your code architecture decisions
        Review your past projects and be ready to discuss trade-offs made

    Behavioral Questions:
        Prepare stories about challenging technical problems you've solved
        Examples of mentoring junior developers or leading technical decisions
        Times you've had to learn new technologies quickly
        Conflict resolution and collaboration experiences

    Mock Technical Discussions:
        Practice explaining complex technical concepts simply
        Be ready to discuss pros/cons of different architectural approaches
        Prepare to walk through how you'd design a system from scratch
        Practice code reviews and discussing code quality
